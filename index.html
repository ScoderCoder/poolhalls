The Facts

  • This website is set in a time where certain Indigenous peoples could be banned from pool halls by a court's decision.
  • The news section depicts real events. Informal pressure from the government in 1927 started this regulation.
  • The 1930 ammendment formally brought this rule, which is punishable. This will be explained in more depth throughout the website.

A Short Timeline

  • 1927: Unofficial bans begin throughout the nation.
  • 1930: The Indian Act is ammended to include this rule, including bans & penalties.
  • 1985: This power remains, however now in the hands of the governor general.
